The Adventure Skills Hill Walking Team will be hosting Scouting Irelands 2020 ‘Mountain Moot’

on the Weekend of the 13th to 15th November at the An Óige Errigal Hostel in Dunlewey, Co Donegal.

https://anoige.ie/errigal-youth-hostel/#Overview

The Cost is still €100 (Yes, Still)

And for that €100 you will get 2 nights in an en-suite bedroom.

It will cover all the food you will need. Friday supper, Saturday Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Gala Dinner. Sunday Breakfast & Lunch

Expert tuition for all of Saturday, a Guide to take you off the hill in the dark. Workshops on Sunday.

The Mountain Moot is a weekend offering a combination of hill training and skills development with workshops and hill skill masterclasses,

Not forgetting old fashioned hiking and good camaraderie against the wonderful backdrop of the Donegal Mountains.

This weekend is open to any adult member of Scouting Ireland, regardless of skill levels and

qualifications as we believe that ‘hillwalking is for everybody’

.

You don’t have to know anything about hillwalking to join us

However, to help participants to get the most out of the weekend all participants should be able to walk a minimum of 6 km with a daypack up & down hills over broken ground.

There will be some walking (under instruction/supervision) in the dark and we may also be walking in bad weather.

The Hill skills team are trying to broaden the skills base of the association and are targeting a

minimum group participation of 20 groups which means we must limit the numbers per group attending to 3

The Mountain Moot in Donegal is now available for you to book via the Membership Services website found at my.scouts.ie

It's open to the first 60 people. So you need to be quick!

Non paid bookings will auto cancel after a short time and will be available for anyone else to book

If this is your first time to book an event through my.scouts.ie then read this link first

https://www.scouts.ie/T-C/User-Manuals/

The ONLY way to book is through my.scouts.ie.

Minimum age of participants is 18

All participants MUST have completed their Garda Vetting and Safeguarding (or safeguarding refresh) within the previous 3 years of the 15th November'20.

If you encounter a problem booking or have a query regarding payment or refunds please contact National Office on (01)4956300 or questions@scouts.ie
